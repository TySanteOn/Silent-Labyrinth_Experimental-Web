Silent Labrinth

You wake up in a maze, completely in the dark and alone...at least you hope so.

Light your way by making a loud noise, it's the only way to get a sense of your surroundings...

You'll need to find and collect three keys to open the exit of the maze, if you even make it there...

Don't be too loud, things are lurking around, they might not see you but they can hear very well...

Use the UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT keys to move around, hit the SPACEBAR to start the game
